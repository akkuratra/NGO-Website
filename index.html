An Initiative of Housewives




Education is the movement from darkness to light

Education is the best weapon to defeat the evils that prevail in the society. With this belief, Navkiran started with a humble beginning of tutoring 4 kids from underprivileged society in 2011. Understanding the needs and importance for these kids Navkiran continued with a tutoring model till 2017 with numbers of course grown significantly. Led by housewifes this initiative got formalized in 2017 forming by a "Not for Profit" society "Navkiran Educational & Welfare Society". It requires your support to continue to bring these kids into mainstream and make a better society which has opportunities for all.

The sole purpose is to quality education accessible to underprivileged kids along with awareness and professional advice on health, hygiene and moral values. It provides free education, uniforms, books and eatables to these kids.

Nikita Bansal

PROJECT OWNER


Nikita Bansal
Age: 47 yrs
Occupation: Housewife
City: Dehradun, India
As an effort to contribute my bit towards society I am tutoring about 30-35 such children every year at my home during afternoon without any charges for last seven years. With this effort their results have improved significantly. The children, who were failing in their classes and their parents were on the verge of withdrawing their names from school, are amongst the top 10 in their class. They are upbeat about studies and also there is noticeable change in their personality. Appreciating this significant improvement in children more and more such parents are willing to send their children for these make up classes.
