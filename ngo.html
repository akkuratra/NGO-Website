Education is the movement from darkness to light An Initiative Of Housewives Your support will build our future


" Education is the best weapon to defeat the evils that prevail in the society. With this belief, Navkiran started
with a humble beginning of tutoring 4 kids from underprivileged society in 2011. Understanding the needs and importance for these kids
Navkiran continued with a tutoring model till 2017 with numbers of course grown significantly. Led by housewifes this initiative got formalized
in 2017 forming by a "Not for Profit" society "Navkiran Educational & Welfare Society". It requires your support to continue to bring these kids
into mainstream and make a better society which has opportunities for all."
